Plot Summary

Two imprisoned men bond over a number of years, finding solace and eventual redemption through acts of common decency. Andy Dufresne, a banker sentenced to life in Shawshank State Penitentiary for the murder of his wife and her lover, maintains his innocence while forming a friendship with Ellis "Red" Redding, a contraband smuggler.
